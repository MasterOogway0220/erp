    10. Mandatory System Controls (Non-Negotiable)
    Role-based access control
    Auto document numbering
    Audit trail for every edit
    No deletion of approved records
    Mandatory attachments (MTC, Inspection)
    
    
    
    11. UAT Acceptance Checklist (Extract)
    Enquiry traceable till invoice
    Heat No. traceable till dispatch
    NCR linked to corrective action
    MIS generated without manual input
