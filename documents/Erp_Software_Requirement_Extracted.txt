


    
    ERP Software Requirement Specification
    
    For Trading Company – Oil, Gas, Power &amp; Petrochemical Industries
    
    
    1. Objective of the Software
    The objective of this software is to develop an integrated ERP system for a trading company dealing in Stainless Steel, Carbon Steel, Alloy Steel, Nickel &amp; Copper Alloys, Tubulars, Structurals, Pipe Fittings, Flanges, Valves, Instrumentation, Mechanical &amp; Electrical Components for Oil, Gas, Power &amp; Petrochemical Industries.
    The system should: - Control end-to-end business processes - Ensure traceability from enquiry to delivery &amp; payment - Support quality assurance &amp; documentation - Generate MIS for management decisions - Fully comply with ISO 9001:2018 Quality Management System requirements
    
    
    
    2. General System Requirements (Common Across All Modules)
    These points must be communicated clearly to the software development team.
    
    2.1 User &amp; Access Control
    Role-based access (Admin, Sales, Purchase, QC, Stores, Accounts, Management)
    User-wise activity logging (audit trail)
    Password policy &amp; login history
    
    
    2.2 Master Data Management
    Customer Master
    Supplier/Vendor Master
    Product Master (Material grade, size, standard, schedule, heat no., manufacturer)
    Tax Master (GST, IGST, export tax)
    Unit of Measure (Nos, Mtrs, Kg, MT)
    Payment terms, delivery terms, inspection agency master
    
    
    2.3 Document Control (ISO 9001 Clause 7.5)
    Auto document numbering
    Revision control
    Approval workflows
    Controlled templates for quotations, POs, invoices, MTCs
    Soft copy storage with version history
    
    
    2.4 Traceability (Critical for ISO 9001)
    Traceability from:
    Enquiry → Quotation → Sales Order → Purchase Order → Incoming Inspection → Inventory → Dispatch → Invoice → Payment
    Heat No. / Batch No. level traceability
    
    
    
    
    3. Module-wise Detailed Requirements
    
    
    3.1 Quotation Management Module
    
    Functional Requirements
    Enquiry registration (customer, project, item-wise)
    Multi-item quotations
    Support for multiple materials &amp; standards
    Costing breakup:
    Material cost
    Logistics
    Inspection
    Margin
    Validity period control
    Revision of quotations with version history
    Approval before release
    
    
    Outputs
    Professional quotation format (PDF &amp; email)
    Comparison of quotation vs sales order
    
    
    ISO 9001 Alignment
    Clause 8.2 (Customer requirements)
    Approved quotation as controlled document
    
    
    
    
    3.2 Inventory Management Module
    
    Functional Requirements
    Warehouse &amp; location-wise inventory
    Heat No./Batch-wise stock
    Incoming material entry against PO
    Stock status:
    Under inspection
    Accepted
    Rejected
    Reserved for SO
    FIFO / Heat-based issue
    Minimum stock level alerts
    
    
    ISO 9001 Alignment
    Clause 8.5.4 (Preservation of product)
    Clause 8.5.2 (Identification &amp; traceability)
    
    
    
    
    3.3 Sales Order Management Module
    
    Functional Requirements
    Sales order creation from approved quotation
    Partial dispatch &amp; back-order tracking
    Link SO with inventory reservation
    Delivery schedule tracking
    Packing list &amp; dispatch note generation
    
    
    ISO 9001 Alignment
    Clause 8.5 (Production &amp; service provision)
    Customer delivery performance tracking
    
    
    
    
    3.4 Purchase Order Management Module
    
    Functional Requirements
    Purchase requisition generation (manual / auto from SO)
    PO creation with technical specifications
    Vendor-wise PO history
    PO amendments with revision control
    Delivery follow-up &amp; delay alerts
    
    
    ISO 9001 Alignment
    Clause 8.4 (Control of externally provided processes, products &amp; services)
    Approved vendor linkage
    
    
    
    
    3.5 Quality Control &amp; Mill Test Certificate (MTC) Module
    
    Functional Requirements
    Incoming material inspection entry
    Inspection checklist (chemical, mechanical, visual, dimensional)
    Inspection status (Accepted / Rejected / Hold)
    Non-Conformance Report (NCR) generation
    MTC upload from mill
    Auto linking of MTC with heat no., PO &amp; SO
    Client-specific MTC format
    
    
    ISO 9001 Alignment
    Clause 8.6 (Release of products &amp; services)
    Clause 8.7 (Control of nonconforming outputs)
    Clause 7.5 (Documented information)
    
    
    
    
    3.6 Invoice &amp; Payment Management Module
    
    Functional Requirements
    Invoice creation from dispatch
    GST compliance (India)
    Export invoice format support
    Credit note / Debit note
    Receipt entry against invoice
    Outstanding &amp; ageing reports
    TDS &amp; bank reconciliation support
    
    
    ISO 9001 Alignment
    Clause 8.5.1 (Service provision control)
    Financial traceability
    
    
    
    
    3.7 MIS &amp; Reports Module
    
    Standard MIS Reports
    Sales vs Target
    Quotation success ratio
    Inventory ageing
    Vendor performance
    Customer payment ageing
    NCR &amp; rejection analysis
    On-time delivery (OTD)
    
    
    Management Dashboard
    Real-time KPIs
    Graphical reports
    
    
    ISO 9001 Alignment
    Clause 9.1 (Monitoring, measurement, analysis &amp; evaluation)
    Clause 9.3 (Management review)
    
    
    
    
    4. ISO 9001:2018 Specific Mandatory Requirements to Inform Developer
    The software must support the following ISO clauses:
    Clause 4.4: Process interaction mapping
    Clause 5.3: Roles &amp; responsibilities
    Clause 6.1: Risk identification (delivery delay, supplier delay)
    Clause 7.2: Competence (user role mapping)
    Clause 7.5: Documented information control
    Clause 8: Operational planning &amp; control
    Clause 9: Internal audit &amp; MIS
    Clause 10: Corrective action tracking
    
    
    
    5. Audit &amp; Compliance Features
    Complete audit trail for every transaction
    Change history with user, date &amp; reason
    Data export for ISO audit
    Access to historical records (minimum 5–7 years)
    
    
    
    6. Technology &amp; Security Recommendations
    Web-based ERP (cloud or on-premise)
    Database with backup &amp; disaster recovery
    SSL encryption
    Daily automated backups
    API support for future integrations
    
    
    
    7. Deliverables Expected from Software Development Team
    Detailed Functional Requirement Document (FRD)
    Database schema
    UI/UX mockups
    ISO compliance mapping document
    User manuals
    Training &amp; support plan
    
    
    
    8. Conclusion
    This ERP software should not only manage business operations but also act as a Quality Management System (QMS) support tool, ensuring compliance with ISO 9001:2018 and providing full traceability, control, and continuous improvement capabilities.
